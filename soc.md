# SOC Roles Introduction

**Objective:** Understand cybersecurity team structures and triage incidents.

**Concepts Covered:**
- **Blue Team (SOC):** First line of defense. Analyzes threats and configures security tools.
- **CIRT/CSIRT/CERT:** Incident responders. Firefighters called when incidents escalate.
- **Red Team & GRC:** Offensive testing and governance/compliance.
- **Internal SOC:** Defends a single organization (e.g., a bank). Steady workload.
- **MSSP:** Outsourced security provider. Defends multiple clients. High alert volume.
